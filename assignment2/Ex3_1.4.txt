// +-----------+-------------------------------------+

// +-----------+-------------------------------------+

// | 0x02  [2] | operand 2                           |

// | 0x04  [4] | result MSB                          |
// | 0x05  [5] | counter                             |
// | 0x06  [6] | MSB of the operand 1 (used for sum) |
// +-----------+-------------------------------------+
